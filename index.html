Why We Do This

HALo was born in 2023 purely and simply out of a son’s love for his mother...

For a number of years, I saw my Mum’s health deteriorate as Altzheimer’s took its toll on her mind. It was clear to my brother and I that her relatively low maintenance property was too much for her to cope with on her own and we found ourselves facing the very difficult decision concerning Mum’s long term care.

Mum was adamant that she didn’t want to leave the reassuring familiarity of her home environment. I knew that to forcing her to move would take a huge toll on her both mentally and emotionally, but at the same time, we couldn’t escape the painful reality that her home had become too much for her to manage and that, as a result, she was no longer taking proper care of herself.

Why choose HALO?

At HALO we understand that trust is earned not given.

At every level of the organisation we undertake to strive for excellence. We offer our people the most generous and flexible working arrangements possible and believe that if we treat our people with kindness and compassion, this will be reflected in the culture of care which they provide to you and your loved ones.

We take pride in our commitment to providing a safe and comfortable environment for our clients, and we are dedicated to ensuring that they receive the highest quality assistance possible.

Introduction to our Services

Welcome to our services page. We are pleased to offer a variety of packages to meet your needs, ranging from 4 to 20 hours. Our packages are designed to provide you with a variety of services that can help improve your quality of life and make your daily routine easier.

THRIVE

Joining the HALO Family

When you meet with our team - here's what you will experience

At HALO, we believe that everyone deserves to live their life with purpose and dignity. That's why, after conducting a thorough assessment, we work closely with you to create a personalised service package that meets your unique needs and helps you maintain the independence and comfort of the home you love.

We want to meet you at home, preferable with your loved ones where we will conduct a three part assessment.

Home Safety Assesment

We want to make sure that your home provides you and our employees and our home assistants with a safe and secure environment.

Needs Assesment

We will determine the areas where you ro your loved ones require assistance, whether that be your common activities of daily living such as dressing, cooking and moving from a chair or bed. We can then assess if these activities need support or can be performed independently.

Enhancements

Our team will also work with you to determine the areas of your property that require attention, whether it's your garden or other aspects of your home. The enhancements assessment will generally identify some recurring work required to the home which will make your life easier. Fitting grab handles, mobility ramps, stair lifts, other mobility aids, or repurposing rooms as necessary to create a safe and secure home environment for you.

The Services We Provide

Find out what you can expect from our services

Whether you need assistance with household tasks or are looking for someone to spend time with, we have you covered. Below is what you can expect from each service.

Companionship Visits

Our companionship visits are perfect for those who may be feeling lonely or isolated. Our friendly and compassionate staff members will spend time with you, providing company and conversation. Whether you enjoy playing cards, reading, or watching a movie, we will tailor our visits to your interests.

Cleaning

Our cleaning services include dusting, vacuuming, and bathroom and kitchen cleaning. We understand that maintaining a clean living environment is important for both physical and mental health, which is why we take pride in our attention to detail and thorough cleaning methods.

Shopping

Our shopping services can be a lifesaver for those who have difficulty leaving the house or carrying heavy bags. We will accompany you to the grocery store or any other shopping destination and help you find the items you need.

Meal Preperation

Help with meal preparation can make a significant difference in your daily routine. We will work with you to create a meal plan that meets your dietary needs and preferences and prepare delicious and nutritious meals for you to enjoy.

Medication Reminders

For those who may have difficulty remembering to take their medication, our medication reminder services can be a lifesaver. Our staff members will remind you when it is time to take your medication and ensure that you are following your prescribed regimen. (Administering perscribe medication is a restricted activity resperved for healthcare profesionals with a certain lvel of qualification.)

Social Outings

Our social outings are designed to help you stay active and engaged in your community. Whether it's a trip to one of our lovely local social centers or a visit to a local place of beauty, our staff members will accompany you and provide you with companionship and support.

Laundry

Either using your own facilities or making use of commercial laundry providers we are happy to take in your laundry bed sheets, linen towels and return them to you need to adveritse tom gret vieweers.

Carers Respite

Whether you need time to take care of personal business, attend to your own health needs, or simply relax, our Carers Respite service can help. Our team of highly trained and compassionate professionals can provide in-home support to your loved one, giving you the time you need to rechargAt e and come back refreshed.

Home Maintenance

Our skilled professionals are equipped with the necessary tools and equipment to ensure that your property is well-maintained and in good condition. We can arrange for decorating and changes to the home on a one off basis.

Gardening

We appreciate that you get a lot of enjoyment from your garden and our gardening team will be pleased to mow your lawns weed your borders cut back your hedges.

Handyman Services

We do not want you or your loved ones climbing on chairs to change lightbulbs or rehang curtains. Our handymen offer a wide range of services such as pressure washing, gutter cleaning, hanging pictures, curtains or shelve.

You tell us...

We strive to be flexible to your needs and if there is something that you need to keep you in your home which we have not listed here then we will be pleased to consider any specific needs you may have. Help with letter writing, making appointments and other family office tasks. Our home assistants do not handle client moneys as a matter of corporate.

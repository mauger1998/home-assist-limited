Joining the HALo Family

When you meet with our team - here's what you will experience

At HALo, we believe that everyone deserves to live their life with purpose and dignity. That's why, after conducting a thorough assessment, we work closely with you to create a personalised service package that meets your unique needs and helps you maintain the independence and comfort of the home you love.

We want to meet you at home, preferably with your loved ones where we will conduct a free assessment.

Personal Needs Assessment

Our personalised assessment process begins with a comprehensive evaluation of your unique needs and preferences. During this assessment, Gina or Fiona will meet with you or your loved one to discuss various aspects of daily life, health, and personal preferences. We'll listen attentively to your concerns, gaining insight into your specific challenges, goals, and areas where you may require assistance.

The assessment covers a wide range of topics, including but not limited to:

  • Physical health status and any medical conditions
  • Mobility and functional abilities
  • Daily routines and activities of daily living (ADLs)
  • Cognitive abilities and any cognitive impairments, such as dementia
  • Emotional well-being and social engagement
  • Medication management and any special dietary needs
  • Home environment and safety considerations

    • Based on the information gathered during the assessment, we'll collaborate with you and your family to develop a customised care plan that addresses your individual needs and promotes your independence, dignity, and overall well-being. Our goal is to provide you with the highest quality of care and support, tailored to your unique circumstances and preferences.

Property Needs assessment

Our team can also work with you to determine the areas of your property that require attention, whether it's your garden or other aspects of your home. If you are unfortunately in a situation where you are now having to manage the maintenance of a property, please ask us and we will be happy to help.

The Services We Provide

Find out what you can expect from our services

Whether you need assistance with personal care, household tasks or are looking for someone to spend time with, we have you covered. Below is what you can expect from each service:

Personal Care

Our experienced caregivers excel in supporting individuals with a range of personal hygiene tasks, from showers to soothing baths or gentle strip washes. We deeply value the preservation of dignity and privacy, particularly when attending to toileting and incontinence requirements. With empathy and professionalism, our compassionate team ensures every interaction is marked by sensitivity and utmost respect.

Platinum Stairlifts

HALo is proud to be an official agent for Platinum Stairlifts, a leading manufacturer of high-quality stairlift solutions since 1998. Designed for comfort, safety, and ease of use, Platinum Stairlifts offer a seamless way to regain full access to your home.

Autumn Care Package (Coming Soon)

Experience complete peace of mind with our dedicated Halo Bearer living in the comfort of your home, providing unparalleled support and personalised care. Trust us to deliver a level of assistance comparable to nursing homes, but within the familiar surroundings of your own space.

Companionship Visits

Our companionship visits are perfect for those who may be feeling lonely or isolated. Our friendly and compassionate staff members will spend time with you, providing company and conversation. Our staff are trained in dementia awareness and can tailor their support to the person's needs.

Cleaning

Our cleaning services include bathroom and kitchen cleaning. Hoovering, changing bed sheets and taking the bins out. We understand that maintaining a clean-living environment is important for both physical and mental health, which is why we take pride in our attention to detail and thorough cleaning methods.

Shopping

Our shopping services can be a lifesaver for those who have difficulty leaving the house or carrying heavy bags. We will accompany you to the grocery store or any other shopping destination and help you find the items you need.

Meal Preparation

Help with meal preparation can make a significant difference in your daily routine. We will work with you to create a meal plan that meets your dietary needs and preferences and prepare delicious and nutritious meals for you to enjoy.

Medication

We offer comprehensive assistance with medication management, encompassing prompting, preparation, and administration.

Social Outings

Our social outings are designed to help you stay active and engaged in your community. Whether it's a trip to one of our lovely local social centers or a visit to a local place of beauty, our staff members will accompany you and provide you with companionship and support.

Laundry

We take care of your laundry needs right in the comfort of your home, guaranteeing fresh clothes, clean bed sheets, and ironing services.

Carers Respite

Whether you need time to take care of personal business, attend to your own health needs, or simply relax, our Carers Respite service can help. Our team of highly trained and compassionate professionals can provide in-home support to your loved one, giving you the time, you need to recharge and come back refreshed.

Home Maintenance

Our skilled professionals are equipped with the necessary tools and equipment to ensure that your property is well-maintained and in good condition. We can arrange for decorating and changes to the home on a one-off basis.

Gardening

We understand that your garden is a place of enjoyment and relaxation. Our experienced gardening team is here to help keep it looking its best, offering services such as lawn mowing, weeding, hedge trimming, and general garden maintenance. Whether you need regular upkeep or a one-time tidy-up, we take care of the hard work so you can continue to enjoy your outdoor space.

Handyman Services

Our handymen offer a wide range of services such as pressure washing, gutter cleaning, hanging pictures, curtains, or shelves, and installing grab rails for assistance.

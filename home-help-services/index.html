Home Assist
Limited's Services

Empowering people to live fulfilling lives in their own homes through the provision of personalised assistance delivered with kindness and compassion.

Introduction to our Services

Welcome to our services page. We are pleased to offer a variety of packages to meet your needs, ranging from 4 to 20 hours. Our packages are designed to provide you with a variety of services that can help improve your quality of life and make your daily routine easier.

The Services We Provide

Find out what you can expect from our services

Whether you need assistance with household tasks or are looking for someone to spend time with, we have you covered. Below is what you can expect from each service.

Companionship Visits

Our companionship visits are perfect for those who may be feeling lonely or isolated. Our friendly and compassionate staff members will spend time with you, providing company and conversation. Whether you enjoy playing cards, reading, or watching a movie, we will tailor our visits to your interests.

Cleaning

Our cleaning services include dusting, vacuuming, and bathroom and kitchen cleaning. We understand that maintaining a clean living environment is important for both physical and mental health, which is why we take pride in our attention to detail and thorough cleaning methods.

Shopping

Our shopping services can be a lifesaver for those who have difficulty leaving the house or carrying heavy bags. We will accompany you to the grocery store or any other shopping destination and help you find the items you need.

Meal Prepartion

Help with meal preparation can make a significant difference in your daily routine. We will work with you to create a meal plan that meets your dietary needs and preferences and prepare delicious and nutritious meals for you to enjoy.

Medication Reminders

For those who may have difficulty remembering to take their medication, our medication reminder services can be a lifesaver. Our staff members will remind you when it is time to take your medication and ensure that you are following your prescribed regimen.

Social Outings

Our social outings are designed to help you stay active and engaged in your community. Whether it's a trip to one of our lovely local social centers or a visit to a local place of beauty, our staff members will accompany you and provide you with companionship and support.

Carers Respite Service

As a private carer, it's important to take some time for yourself to rest and recharge.

At HALo, we understand the demanding nature of caregiving and the impact it can have on a carer's well-being. That's why we offer Carers Respite, a service designed to support carers and give them the break they deserve.

Whether you need time to take care of personal business, attend to your own health needs, or simply relax, our Carers Respite service can help. Our team of highly trained and compassionate professionals can provide in-home support to your loved one, giving you the time you need to rechargAt e and come back refreshed.

Maintenance Bolt on Packages

Our company offers a range of 2-4 hours a week packages that are focused on the maintenance and upkeep of your property.

Our skilled professionals are equipped with the necessary tools and equipment to ensure that your property is well-maintained and in good condition. Our bolt-on maintenance package includes a wide range of services such as gardening, jet washing, light maintenance work such as painting, gutter clearing, and much more. Our experts are trained to cater to your specific needs and preferences, ensuring that the work is carried out to the highest standard.

How Does It Work

When you meet with our head of care - here's what you may experience:

We will conduct a thorough assessment by asking several questions to determine the areas where you, or your loved one may require assistance, as well as identify the aspects of their daily life that are currently functioning smoothly.

We will inquire about the common Activities of Daily Living such as bathing, dressing, cooking, and moving from a chair or bed. They will assess whether these activities can still be performed independently or if support is needed.

Once we have completed our assessment, we will propose care options based on any identified gaps or deficiencies. The home will be inspected to assess any safety hazards, including trip hazards around the house.

HOME ASSIST LIMITED

Letter From Our Chairman

"Empowering people to live fulfilling lives in their own homes through the provision of personalised assistance delivered with kindness and compassion."

Chairmans Letter

For several years, I watched as my Mum’s health deteriorated under the weight of Alzheimer’s disease. It wasn’t just her memory that suffered; her ability to care for herself also began to decline. Preparing meals, managing her medication, looking after her property and attending to personal care had all become overwhelming for her, and my brother and I faced the heart-wrenching decision about Mum’s long-term care.

Mum was adamant that she didn’t want to leave the reassuring familiarity of her home. She had built so many memories there, and it was her sanctuary. I knew that forcing her to move would take a huge toll on her mental and emotional well-being, but we couldn’t ignore the fact that she was struggling to look after herself properly.

When we looked for solutions, we encountered many excellent nursing care providers. However, Mum didn’t need nursing care yet—she needed a helping hand with daily living. Someone to assist her with meals, ensure she took her medication, help her with personal care like showering, and offer her meaningful companionship.

Put simply, I wanted to help my Mum remain in her home and live with the dignity she has always carried.

This idea became the foundation of Home Assist Limited.

Soon after, I realised that other families faced similar challenges. People began reaching out to me, asking if I could extend these services to their loved ones. Their encouragement led to the creation of HALo—a vision born from my love for my Mum and a desire to help others in similar circumstances.

HALo is committed to empowering people to live fulfilling lives in their homes through the provision of personalised assistance delivered with kindness and compassion.

At every level of the organisation, we strive for excellence. We offer our people the most generous and flexible working arrangements possible and believe that if we treat our people with kindness and compassion, this will be reflected in the culture of care which they provide to you and your loved ones.

HALo was born out of family love, and we pledge to bring this same kindness and compassion to you and your loved ones, helping them to stay in the home of their choice with the dignity and respect they deserve for as long as it is safe to do so.

PLATINUM STAIRLIFTS

Regain Full Access to Your Home with Platinum Stairlifts

Comfortable, safe, and built to fit your staircase

Custom built for your home and expertly installed in Guernsey, our stairlifts offer comfort, safety, and peace of mind, every step of the way.

HALo is proud to be an official agent for Platinum Stairlifts

If stairs have started to feel like a challenge, a stairlift can help you move around your home with confidence and comfort. Designed and built in the North of England, Platinum Stairlifts are trusted around the world for their quality, safety, and long-term reliability.

Every stairlift is custom built to suit your space, and we handle the full installation and aftercare locally in Guernsey, so you can enjoy peace of mind and support close to home.

Why choose a Platinum Stairlift through HALo?

Whether you live alone or with family, maintaining access to your full home matters. HALo works with Platinum Stairlifts to offer tailored solutions that work for a wide range of homes, mobility levels, and personal preferences.

With several models to choose from, and optional features such as powered swivels or forward-facing travel, we’ll help you select the option that suits you best.

BENEFITS

Built for you

Choice & Custom Fit

Choose from three expertly engineered models, each one crafted to suit straight or curved staircases, narrow spaces, or more complex layouts. Options include custom seat sizes, powered hinges, and a wide range of upholstery finishes.

Innovation

The Platinum Ultimate offers forward-facing travel, perfect for those who may struggle with bending their knees or turning in a seated position. It also offers a first-step start option, removing the need for the rail to extend into your hallway.

Comfort

You’ll use your stairlift daily, so comfort matters. From padded, ergonomically designed seating to simple, easy-to-use controls, everything is designed to make each journey smooth and enjoyable.

Safety

Every stairlift is rigorously tested before it leaves the warehouse and comes with a signed certificate of quality. That means you can feel safe and confident every time you sit down.

Space-Saving Design

All models come with folding arms, footrests and seats to keep your staircase clear when not in use. You can also choose rail features that minimise hallway obstruction and fit neatly into your space.

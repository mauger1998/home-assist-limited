HOME ASSIST LIMITED

About Home
Assist Limited

At HALo, we believe that everyone deserves to live a happy and fulfilled life in the home of their choice and we are dedicated to providing compassionate and reliable support to help you or your loved ones achieve just that.

PURPOSE

Our Mission

Our mission is simple...

Empowering people to live fulfilling lives in their own homes through the provision of personalised assistance delivered with kindness and compassion.

CELEBRATE

The HALo Family

The HALo family is in three parts:

Management People

Our management people are always available for you. Our managers are your first point of contact and will conduct the initial and periodic home assessments to ensure the package you receive is optimised for your ever changing needs.

House People

Our house people are handymen, gardeners, and maintenance professionals. After the initial assessment, they will propose ongoing routine maintenance tasks to keep your home in optimal condition so that you get the maximum benefit from living there.

People People

Once the assistance package has been determined your home assistant will become your principal contact, they will lead the service delivery to keep you in your home. They are committed to delivering the highest quality in-home support service.

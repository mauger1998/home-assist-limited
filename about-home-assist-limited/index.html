HOME ASSIST LIMITED

About Home
Assist Limited

At HALo, we believe that everyone deserves to live a happy and fulfilled life in the home of their choice and we are dedicated to providing compassionate and reliable support to help you or your loved ones achieve just that.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple...

Empowering people to live fulfilling lives in their own homes through the provision of personalised assistance delivered with kindness and compassion.

Total Piece Of Mind With The App

Experience total peace of mind with the OnCare app, an integral part of our care approach. Through our collaboration with OnCare, we harness the power of technology to create personalised care plans, enhance communication with families, and ensure efficient organization of important information, such as doctor's appointments and caregiver updates. With OnCare, you'll have a comprehensive solution that brings ease, clarity, and unity to your loved one's care journey.

The HALo Family

The HALo family is in three parts:

Management

Our management team is dedicated to delivering exceptional care and seamless operations at HALo.

Gina, our Managing Director, oversees all aspects of the business, from the initial client inquiry to onboarding, ensuring a smooth and supportive start to your HALo journey.

Fiona, our Director of Nursing Operations and a Registered Nurse, leads our caregiving team with expertise and compassion. She upholds the highest standards of care, develops tailored care plans, conducts assessments, and advocates for client safety.

Nikita, our Coordinator, manages the daily scheduling and coordination of staff, ensuring efficient communication between clients and caregivers. She handles client inquiries, sends out weekly schedules, and assists with any requests regarding visits.

Support Assistants

Our Support Assistants are the heart of HALo, bringing compassion, professionalism, and a wealth of experience to every client they support. Dedicated to delivering the highest standard of care, our team goes beyond just assisting with daily tasks—they build meaningful connections, promote independence, and ensure dignity in every interaction.

Each of our caregivers is carefully selected, highly trained, and continuously supported through ongoing professional development. We invest in their skills and well-being, ensuring they have the knowledge and confidence to provide exceptional care tailored to individual needs.

Whether offering companionship, personal care, or complex support, our Support Assistants are committed to making a real difference in the lives of our clients. With HALo, you can trust that you or your loved one will receive compassionate, expert care from a team that truly cares.

Property Maintenance & Stairlifts

Robert is a skilled and experienced professional dedicated to helping clients maintain safe, comfortable, and accessible homes. With years of hands-on expertise, he has installed countless home aids, including bespoke rails, grab bars, and other essential modifications to enhance mobility and independence.

Beyond home adaptations, Rob provides reliable general maintenance and garden care, ensuring outdoor spaces remain tidy and enjoyable. Whether it’s small repairs, accessibility improvements, or keeping your garden in top shape, Rob approaches every task with care and precision.

Rob is also our stairlift expert, working in partnership with Platinum Stairlifts to provide high-quality mobility solutions. From professional installation to ongoing support, he ensures that every stairlift is fitted with safety, comfort, and ease of use in mind—helping clients regain full access to their homes.
